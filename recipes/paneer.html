SHAHI PANEER RECIPE


Home



DESCRIPTION

To make **Shahi Paneer**, start by heating oil or ghee in a pan and sautéing chopped onions, cashews, and tomatoes until soft. Let it cool, then blend into a smooth paste. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté ginger-garlic paste with aromatic spices like cardamom and cinnamon. Add the blended mixture and cook until the oil separates. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala, then pour in fresh cream and milk for richness. Add paneer cubes and let them simmer in the creamy gravy. Adjust salt and garnish with coriander leaves or nuts. Serve hot with naan or rice.


INGREDIENTS

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cashews
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Spices: cardamom, cinnamon, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Naan or rice for serving

STEPS

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and sauté chopped onions, cashews, and tomatoes until soft.
  2. Let the mixture cool, then blend into a smooth paste.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté ginger-garlic paste with aromatic spices.
  4. Add the blended mixture and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala.
  6. Pour in fresh cream and milk for richness.
  7. Add paneer cubes and let them simmer in the creamy gravy.
  8. Adjust salt and garnish with coriander leaves or nuts.
  9. Serve hot with naan or rice.
